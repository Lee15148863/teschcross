📱 Mobile Repair Warranty & Disclaimer

Please read carefully before submitting your device for repair.

1. Repair Warranty

Repaired or replaced parts are covered under a 90-day warranty (industry standard).

Warranty covers only the specific part repaired or replaced and does not include:

  • Other unrelated faults
  • Accidental or physical damage after repair
  • Liquid damage after repair
  • Any other external damage

2. Water Damage Repair

Devices with water damage are complex, and repairs cannot be guaranteed.

Certain sensitive components (e.g., Face ID, fingerprint sensors, cameras) may be permanently damaged and unrecoverable.

3. Hidden or Additional Faults

Liquid damage, internal faults, or damage caused by drops/impact may result in additional faults during or after repair.

Some issues may not be detectable before opening the device; repair may reveal hidden faults or cause new failures.

4. Motherboard Repair Scope & Additional Costs

Quoted prices for water-damaged devices cover motherboard repair only.

Other faulty components (screen, charging port, microphone, speaker, battery, etc.) require separate charges, including parts costs.

5. Data Responsibility

Most data may remain intact, but data loss can occur.

Customers are strongly advised to back up important data before submitting their device.

We cannot guarantee data preservation.

6. Waterproofing

Opening or repairing a device voids its original water resistance.

7. Device Condition

Fragile or previously damaged components may fail during repair.

8. Impact on Manufacturer Warranty

Any inspection or repair by us will void the original manufacturer warranty.

9. Repair Outcome and Customer Agreement

Some devices may not be repairable or may develop additional faults during the repair process.

By submitting your device for repair or diagnostic testing, you acknowledge and agree to all terms and conditions outlined in this warranty and disclaimer.

⚡ Additional Policies

9.1 No Fix, No Fee

If the device cannot be repaired, the customer will not be charged.

9.2 Long-Term Unclaimed Devices (60 Days)

Devices not collected within 60 days may be disposed of or recycled. Customers are responsible for collecting their devices on time.

9.3 Potential Additional Issues

Certain faults may appear after repair, beyond our control.
