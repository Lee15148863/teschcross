How to Check Your iPad Model (2 Quick Ways)

📱 Method 1: Check the back of your iPad

  1. Flip your iPad over
  2. Look at the small print near the bottom
  3. Find the code starting with A (e.g., A1822, A2602)
  4. This A-number is your iPad model

⚙️ Method 2: Check in Settings (most accurate)

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to General → About
  3. Find Model Name or Model Number
  4. Tap the model number to show the A-number

🍎 Apple Device Repair – Simplified Terms & Conditions

Please read before submitting your device for repair.

  1. Repair Warranty
    All repaired or replaced parts are covered by a 90-day limited warranty. The warranty applies only to the specific part repaired and does not cover accidental or physical damage, liquid damage after repair, software issues, or unrelated faults. Original receipt is required for warranty service.
  2. Data Responsibility
    Although we take care when handling devices, data loss may occur during repair. Customers are responsible for backing up all important data before submitting their device. We are not liable for any loss of data.
  3. Apple Component Pairing
    Apple devices use manufacturer component pairing technology. After screen or battery replacement, the device may display messages such as "Important Display Message" or "Important Battery Message". These notifications are introduced by Apple and do not affect device functionality.
  4. Face ID & Sensitive Components
    Face ID sensors and related components are paired to the motherboard. If these components were previously damaged due to drops, liquid exposure, or previous repairs, the feature may not be recoverable.
  5. True Tone Display
    True Tone calibration is linked to the original screen. After screen replacement, True Tone may not always be transferable, but display functionality will remain normal.
  6. Water Resistance
    Opening the device breaks the original factory seal. Water resistance cannot be guaranteed after repair.
  7. Repair Risks
    Devices that have experienced impact damage, liquid damage, or previous repairs may contain hidden faults. Additional issues may appear during or after repair due to existing damage.
  8. No Fix – No Fee
    If the device cannot be repaired, no repair fee will be charged (diagnostic fees may apply).
  9. Uncollected Devices
    Devices not collected within 60 days after notification may be considered abandoned and may be disposed of or recycled.
  10. Acceptance
    By submitting your device for repair, you confirm that you have read and agree to these terms. Please test your device before leaving the store.
