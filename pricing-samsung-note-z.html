How to Check Your Samsung Model

⚙️ Method 1: Check in Settings

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap About phone (or About device)
  3. Find Model name or Model number
  4. Look for the model code (e.g., SM-G998B, SM-S911B)

📱 Method 2: Check the back or SIM tray

  1. Look at the back of your device (older models)
  2. Or remove the SIM tray and check the label
  3. Find the model number starting with SM-
  4. Match it with the model list above
