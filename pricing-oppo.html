How to Check Your OPPO Model

?? Method 1: Check in Settings

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap About phone
  3. Find Model or Device name
  4. Note the model number (e.g., Redmi Note 12, Pixel 8 Pro)

?? Method 2: Check the box or SIM tray

  1. Look at your device's original box
  2. Or remove the SIM tray and check the label
  3. Find the model name printed on the label
  4. Match it with the model list above
