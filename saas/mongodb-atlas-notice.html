1. Purpose

StoreFlow provides software deployment and operation services for retail POS systems. For production use, each merchant may be required to provide and maintain their own MongoDB Atlas database. The merchant's MongoDB Atlas database is separate from StoreFlow's internal administration database and separate from any other merchant's database.

StoreFlow does not sell, transfer, or provide ownership of a MongoDB Atlas account. The merchant must create and manage their own MongoDB Atlas account, database, credentials, access settings, billing, and backup/export procedures.

2. MongoDB Atlas Setup Instructions

Video Tutorial: https://youtube.com/shorts/pIHvoXkwmq4
This video shows the general process of creating a MongoDB Atlas account and setting up a free M0 cluster. MongoDB may update the Atlas interface from time to time, so button names and page layout may vary slightly.

To connect StoreFlow to your own database, you must complete the following steps:

  1. Create a MongoDB Atlas account in your business name or under your lawful business control.
  2. Create a dedicated Atlas database deployment for StoreFlow.
  3. Create a dedicated database user for StoreFlow access. Do not reuse your personal Atlas login password.
  4. Choose a database region suitable for your business and legal requirements. EU/Ireland merchants should normally choose an EU/EEA region unless they have assessed cross-border data transfer requirements.
  5. Configure Network Access in Atlas. For production, use restricted IP access where possible. Avoid 0.0.0.0/0 unless temporary and necessary.
  6. Copy the MongoDB connection string from Atlas and replace <password> with the database user password.
  7. If your username or password contains special characters such as @, :, /, ?, #, %, [, or ], you must encode them correctly in the connection string.
  8. Submit the connection string to StoreFlow only through the secure onboarding form.

3. Free Cluster Warning

MongoDB Atlas Free clusters are suitable for testing, evaluation, or very small-scale use only. A Free cluster has limited storage, limited performance, limited connections, and may not include Atlas backup features.

You must not rely on a Free cluster as the only copy of important trading, transaction, customer, invoice, tax, inventory, or accounting records unless you understand and accept the risk. If you choose to use a Free cluster, you are responsible for regularly exporting or backing up your own data.

StoreFlow strongly recommends that live business users upgrade to an appropriate paid MongoDB Atlas tier and enable backup features where available.

If you are using the free Atlas tier, we recommend that you export your transaction records and back up your data regularly to an independent location.

3. 免费集群重要提醒

MongoDB Atlas 免费集群只适合测试、评估或非常小规模使用。免费集群存储空间、性能、连接数和备份功能都有限制。

如果您把免费集群用于真实门店经营，请不要把它当作唯一的数据保存方式。交易记录、客户信息、发票、税务、库存、会计记录等重要数据，必须由您自行定期导出或备份。

StoreFlow 建议正式营业的商户使用合适的付费 Atlas 等级，并在可用时启用备份功能。

如果您使用免费版 Atlas，我们建议您导出交易记录并备份至独立的安全位置。
