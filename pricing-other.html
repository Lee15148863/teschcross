?? Method 1: Check in Settings

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap About phone
  3. Find Model or Device name
  4. Note the model number (e.g., Redmi Note 12, Pixel 8 Pro)

?? Mobile Repair Warranty & Disclaimer

  1. Repair Warranty
    Repaired or replaced parts are covered under a 90-day warranty (industry standard). Warranty covers only the specific part repaired or replaced and does not include: other unrelated faults, accidental or physical damage after repair, liquid damage after repair, or any other external damage.
  2. Water Damage Repair
    Devices with water damage are complex, and repairs cannot be guaranteed. Certain sensitive components (e.g., Face ID, fingerprint sensors, cameras) may be permanently damaged and unrecoverable.
  3. Hidden or Additional Faults
    Liquid damage, internal faults, or damage caused by drops/impact may result in additional faults during or after repair. Some issues may not be detectable before opening the device; repair may reveal hidden faults or cause new failures.
  4. Motherboard Repair Scope & Additional Costs
    Quoted prices for water-damaged devices cover motherboard repair only. Other faulty components (screen, charging port, microphone, speaker, battery, etc.) require separate charges, including parts costs.
  5. Data Responsibility
    Most data may remain intact, but data loss can occur due to underlying damage or repair complexity. Customers are strongly advised to back up important data. We cannot guarantee data preservation.
  6. Waterproofing
    Opening or repairing a device voids its original water resistance.
  7. Device Condition
    Fragile or previously damaged components may fail during repair.
  8. Impact on Manufacturer Warranty
    Any inspection or repair by us will void the original manufacturer warranty.
  9. Repair Outcome
    Some devices may not be repairable or may develop additional faults during the repair process.

? Additional Policies

No Fix, No Fee: If the device cannot be repaired, the customer will not be charged.

Long-Term Unclaimed Devices (60 Days): Devices not collected within 60 days may be disposed of or recycled. Customers are responsible for collecting their devices on time.

Potential Additional Issues: Certain faults may appear after repair, beyond our control.
