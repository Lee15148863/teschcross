What We Transfer

Our comprehensive data transfer service includes:

  • All contacts with names, phone numbers, and email addresses
  • Photos and videos from your camera roll and albums
  • Apps and their associated data (where possible)
  • Text messages and iMessages
  • Call history and voicemail
  • Calendar events and reminders
  • Notes and documents
  • Music, podcasts, and audiobooks
  • Device settings and preferences
  • Wi-Fi passwords and network settings

⚠️ Important Privacy Notice

We prioritize your privacy and security. However, please note that we are not responsible for any information involving customer privacy, including but not limited to:

  • Banking apps, credit card information, and financial data
  • Password managers and stored passwords
  • Government ID applications and documents
  • Social media account credentials
  • Email account passwords
  • Two-factor authentication apps

You will need to manually log in to these sensitive apps on your new device. We recommend having your passwords ready before the transfer.

How It Works

🚶 Walk-in Service Available: No appointment needed! Simply visit us during business hours with both phones.

Step 1: Bring both your old and new phones to our store.

Step 2: Our technician will assess the condition of both devices.

Step 3: If your old phone screen is broken, we'll perform a temporary repair to enable data access.

Step 4: We'll transfer all your data using professional tools and methods.

Step 5: Verify that all your important data has been successfully transferred.

Estimated transfer time: Starting from 1 hour. The actual time varies depending on the age and brand of your phone. Some models may require longer processing time.
